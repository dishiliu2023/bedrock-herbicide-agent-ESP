Step 3

Crop & Weed Name Resolution (Embeddings)

▾
User-provided crop and weed names are embedded via Amazon Titan Embed Text v2, then matched against pre-computed canonical embeddings using cosine similarity (threshold 0.6). Unmatched weeds trigger a "Did you mean?" suggestion with up to 3 candidates.
